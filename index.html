About Me

As I navigate my journey through the world of computer science at UCSC, I find myself deeply immersed in the art of coding and software engineering. Each day, I dive into the intricacies of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and TypeScript, knowing that every bit of knowledge I gain adds to the foundation of my future career. My passion fuels my perseverance, and I'm constantly inspired by the limitless possibilities that technology offers. I’m not just learning languages or frameworks; I’m honing the skills to create, innovate, and solve problems that will make a lasting impact. I see every challenge as an opportunity to grow, every setback as a chance to learn, and I’m determined to push the boundaries of what I can achieve. With each project, I’m reminded that I’m not just building software—I’m building the future I’ve always dreamed of, one line of code at a time.
